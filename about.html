About Us

Project Details

OppoConnect is a platform dedicated to connecting individuals with diverse needs in Rwanda. It addresses challenges related to job opportunities, job creation, and entrepreneurial growth by providing a centralized platform for job seekers, employers, entrepreneurs, and investors.

Our Team

  • Prince Ndanyuzwe - Project Manager
  • Kevin Nyiringango - Project Coordinator

Mission

Our mission is to foster economic growth in Rwanda by connecting individuals with employment opportunities and supporting entrepreneurial ventures.

Vision

We envision a future where every individual in Rwanda has access to meaningful employment and opportunities for business success.
